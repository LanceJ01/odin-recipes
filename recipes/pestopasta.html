Pesto Pasta

Description

Big dummy's guide to an EASY, FAST, and DELICIOUS pasta!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Pesto
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water
  2. Separate pasta and water once pasta is firm to the bite (aldente)
  3. Mix pasta with pesto
  4. Add parmesean as a topper

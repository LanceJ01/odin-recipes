Air Fried Meatballs

Description

Big dummy's guide to an EASY, FAST, and DELICIOUS Meatballs!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef/pork
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Fresh garlic, salt, & pepper

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients
  2. Shape into balls
  3. Air fry for 8 minutes/li>
